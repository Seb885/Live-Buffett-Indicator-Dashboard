About the Indicator

The "Buffett Indicator" was popularized by Warren Buffett as a high-level measure of broad market valuation. It compares the total value of all publicly traded stocks in the U.S. (Wilshire 5000 index) to the quarterly Gross Domestic Product (GDP).

Guideline:

  • Under 100%: Generally Undervalued
  • 100% - 120%: Fairly Valued
  • Over 120%: Overvalued
